FAQ

Not the usual FAQ page — tap a question to unfold it with a clean, modern motion.

Tip: If you’re not sure what to pick, use “Not sure” in the consultation form.
How does the consultation work?

Fill the request form and it opens a prefilled email. We confirm scope (and any fees) by email before any paid work begins.

What do I get after a consultation?

Clear next steps, key risks, and a recommended plan your team can execute — plus implementation notes when needed.

Can you work with an existing codebase/team?

Yes. We can start with a short audit or targeted fixes, then move into milestones (CI/CD, security hardening, reliability improvements).

Do you do fixed-price or retainers?

Yes — after we confirm scope. Fixed-price works best when deliverables and acceptance criteria are clear.

How do you use AI in your workflow?

To speed up documentation, reviews, checklists, and runbooks — while keeping quality, security, and maintainability first.
